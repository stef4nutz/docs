# Configuring the antiraid system

Preventing mass-joins can be easily done with our antiraid system. Set your own limitations and punishments that you desire.

# Antiraid whitelist

Take this scenario, you or one of your friends have been banned recently off discord and they don't have an alt to join your server and prevent these measurements. Whitelist them so the bot ignores their no-avatar or account aging.
